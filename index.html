SPIE ANDC DU: Hands-on Workshop on Photonics for Student Chapters

The SPIE Student Chapter at Delhi University successfully conducted a comprehensive workshop on advanced photonics techniques, attracting over 200 participants from across the region.

The workshop covered cutting-edge topics including fiber optic communications, laser applications in medicine, and quantum optics fundamentals. Industry experts and academic leaders shared their insights on the latest developments in the field.

Participants had hands-on experience with state-of-the-art equipment and received certificates of participation. The event strengthened industry-academia collaboration and opened new opportunities for student research projects.
